MEET OUR TEAM MEMBERS: GALATHON

From the Information Technology club of Tran Dai Nghia High school for the Gifted, we gathered again to the mutual passion and curiosity towards coding and the dream to share the excitement in our novel virtual world with everyone. In Galathon, our team members are self-motivated individuals from various backgrounds, working together to realize our vision of a survival game with history and culture from different countries at its core.
